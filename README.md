BloodBridge is a web-based platform designed to connect blood donors with patients and hospitals in real time. The system enables users to quickly find available blood donors based on blood group and location, reducing the time required during medical emergencies.
It maintains a database of registered donors and allows users to search, request, and update blood availability efficiently.

The platform also includes an automated email notification feature. When a blood request is generated, registered donors with the matching blood group receive email alerts, enabling faster communication and response. 
This functionality helps ensure that urgent requests reach potential donors immediately.

By addressing challenges such as lack of timely donor information and delays in blood procurement, BloodBridge improves coordination between donors and recipients, 
saves critical time during emergencies, and supports hospitals and communities in managing urgent blood requirements more effectively.
